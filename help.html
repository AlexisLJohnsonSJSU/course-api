Run into any trouble with searching for poems on this app? For the most successful searches, we recommend 1) keeping in mind that most of the poems in our database are older (think Shakespeare) and 2) using only one concept in the topic field (one word).

Let's get you some successful searches so you know what to expect. Here are terms that will definitely bring back poems for you at all lengths:
love
flowers
sun
ire
day
evening
red
cat

But wait! Also, remember that many terms will bring back poems at only one or two of the lengths on offer. So if at first you don't succeed try try another length. Here are some examples that fall into that categorization:
queen - long
shores - medium
night - long
chase - medium
king - short
