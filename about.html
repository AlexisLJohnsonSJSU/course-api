This project was created as a prototype for INFO 246: APIs at SJSU. It's a discovery app for poetry-curious visitors that filters its searches by topics and length.

The topic field searches for matching terms within poems. The length field filters lines of poems by Short (5 lines), Medium (14 lines), and Long (25 lines).

All poems are pulled from the PoetryDB Public API-- visit the site for more information about the poetry database's content scope and API use.

The future updates roadmap for this app could include adding profiles to save favorite poems, options to share poems on social media, and more powerful search parameters.
